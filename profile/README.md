# ✨ DOEdeal!

## 🌟 Bringing People Closer, One Activity at a Time.

Whether it's friends, couples, or the whole family, we make it effortless to discover,
decide on, and book experiences together.

---

## 😩 The Problem

Finding something fun to do with a group is way harder than it should be.

You scroll TikTok, ask Google, dig through Instagram, and eventually land on something
that looks great. Then comes the real challenge: you share it in the WhatsApp group, wait
for replies that never come, get three different opinions, someone says they've already
been there, someone else suggests something completely different, and suddenly the whole
thing falls apart under the weight of too many messages and too little consensus.

Most of the time, you end up doing nothing. At best, you watch TV together.

And on the rare occasion you do agree on something, you still have to hunt for the official website, and figure out how to actually buy tickets.

---

## ✨ The Solution

DOEdeal! centralizes the entire experience — from the first idea to the booking confirmation.

- 🔍 **Discover:** A recommendation algorithm tailored to your interests surfaces activities
  you'll actually want to do, without the endless scrolling.

- 🤝 **Decide together:** Group mode applies the same algorithm across everyone's preferences
  to find common ground automatically. No more WhatsApp threads that go nowhere.

- 🎟️ **Book instantly:** Once you've found something you love, buy tickets directly in the
  app. No redirects, no hunting for links, no leaving the platform.

Everything in one place, so spending quality time together is actually easy.

---

## 📱 The Platform

DOEdeal! is available on **iOS and Android**, with a full web platform on the way.
